Our Story

At Spincy, we believe food is more than fuel — it’s a shared experience, a memory in the making. Our passion for chicken döner is rooted in the rich, diverse streets of Istanbul, where flavor brings people together across cultures and generations. That same spirit lives here in Quincy, where we serve bold, fire-roasted chicken wraps crafted with care, warmth, and just the right spice.

Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite or sitting down with friends, we’re here to turn everyday meals into flavorful moments. Because at Spincy, it’s not just about what’s on the plate — it’s about the feeling it brings.

Our Mission

At Spincy, our mission goes beyond serving great food — we want to nourish our community in every sense. Inspired by the Turkish tradition of "askıda ekmek", we’ve launched the “Wrap It Forward” program: a simple way for customers to donate a meal for someone in need.

Whether you donate $1 or cover an entire wrap, your kindness goes directly to feeding someone who could use a warm, delicious meal.

Because at Spincy, we believe food is a human right — and when we share it, we bring hope, dignity, and connection.
