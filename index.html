Our Story

At Spincy, we believe food is more than fuel — it’s comfort, culture, and connection.

Our love for chicken döner was born in the vibrant streets of Istanbul, where the aroma of fire-roasted meat, the rhythm of busy sidewalks, and the joy of sharing meals with loved ones fills every corner.

That tradition inspired us to bring the same flavor, passion, and hospitality to Quincy, Illinois — a place we now proudly call home.

Every Spincy wrap is handcrafted with marinated chicken, warm lavash, fresh vegetables, and bold, savory sauce — all served with care and love.

Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite or connecting with friends, we’re here to turn every meal into a flavorful moment that brings people together.
